The things I love about Git

I love how nimble you are - I can just 'git init' any directory and it's a repository just like that. I love the way you keep track of files and meticulously show changes in a 'git diff'.

I love the how mad you get when there's merge conflicts; how you make me go through and fix them all before committing anything. I love the how you just can't let go of anything I've done in the past, how a 'git log' will show me all the comits I've made.

And I love the way you keep all your files hidden, but if I go looking for them, I can find them and see what's really going on with you. I love your simple interface, your essential utility, and your whole philosophy of coding.
